About Us

At Rare Beauty, we believe in celebrating individuality and embracing what makes us unique. Founded by Selena Gomez, Rare Beauty is more than just a beauty brand—it's a movement that challenges unrealistic standards of perfection and promotes self-acceptance. Our mission is to break down the mental health stigma, while creating products that make people feel good about themselves, both inside and out. We are dedicated to fostering self-expression without the pressure of conformity. Rare Beauty products are crafted with the aim of enhancing your natural beauty, rather than masking it, with lightweight, breathable formulas that complement all skin types and tones. We are also deeply committed to giving back through the Rare Impact Fund, which helps increase access to mental health services in underserved communities. By joining Rare Beauty, you're not just embracing beauty, but also supporting a cause that aims to create positive change in the world.

This is makeup made to feel good in, without hiding what makes you unique—because Rare Beauty is not about being someone else, but being who you are.
